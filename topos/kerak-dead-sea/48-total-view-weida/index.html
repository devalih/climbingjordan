Total view Weida /Dead Sea

 

1  n.e.           
2  Weida left     4 bolted lines  some trad lines possible   max. 60m  2013    
3  n.e.           
4  Weida  14 bolted routes   3-4 multi - pitches (trad + bolted)   max. 110m   2006 - 2013   
5  Dieu Ludik   4-5b (Trad.) Wilfried Colonna+ Bernard Domenech   200m  2006  
                         
 Please keep the trad. routes clean from any fixed gear!
 

From Amman: Follow Dead Sea road till junction to Kerak, after 5-6 km park on the left side when the huge slab is visible right above the street. (around 1-1,5 hours)

 

Approach: visible from the parking just cross the street and follow small trails to the base of the cliff (around 10min.)

 

 

Orientation: west

Grades: easy - medium

Potential: little more

Rock quality: exellent limestone

Protection: bolted and trad. lines

Gear: 60m single-rope or 60m double-rope, around 10 -15 quick-draws, slings and trad gear
