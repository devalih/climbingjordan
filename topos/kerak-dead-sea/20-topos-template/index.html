Exact lines of Nr 12 + Nr 13 unsure! Look for slings/pitons...

From Amman: Follow Dead sea road till junction to Kerak, after 5-6 km park on the left side when the huge slab is visible right above the street. (around 1-1,5 hours)

 

Approach: visible from the parking just cross the street and follow small trails to the base of the cliff (around 10min.)

 

Orientation: west

Grades: easy - medium

Potential: little more

Rock quality: exellent limestone

Protection: bolted and trad lines

Gear: 60m single-rope or 60m double-rope, around 10 -15 quick-draws, trad.gear,slings

Multi-pitch climbs!
