Please keep this cliff an adventure-place with no bolts – use trad. gear or climb toprope!

 

Orientation: south

Grades: medium

Potential: little more

Rock quality: exellent limestone

Protection: trad. so far

Climbing-meters: 50m

Gear: 50m rope, around 10 quick-draws, nuts + cams

 GPS: 810m N32°24.888´ E35° 44.587´
