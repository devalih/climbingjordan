 Nice cliff close to Amman - therefore the best for fast outdoor climbing hours. 

Short routes with very often quite tough crux-moves!

 

Orientation: east

Grades: easy – medium - hard

Potential: very little more

Rock quality: exellent limestone

Protection: bolted

Gear: 30m single-rope, around 10 quick-draws
