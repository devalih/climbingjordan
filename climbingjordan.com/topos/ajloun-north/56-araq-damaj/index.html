The more interesting western cliff – numerous hard lines possible. The climbing is very technical on a vertical, sometimes overhanging wall. Nice surrounding in a very calm valley. Facing south it is a great winter and spring crag.

Orientation: south

Grades: medium - hard - very hard

Potential:  more

Rock quality: exellent limestone

Protection: trad. + bolted

Climbing-meters: around 275 so far

Gear: 50-60m rope, around 10 quick-draws

GPS: sealevel: 550m N32° 22.599´ E 35° 42.780´
