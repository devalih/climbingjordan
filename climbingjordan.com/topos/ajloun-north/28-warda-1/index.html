Grades: easy - medium - hard

Potential: a lot more

Rock quality: exellent limestone

Protection: Top-anchors are now glued, plates replaced..(how long??) Anyway: Big thanx to MArwan!!!!

Gear: 50-60m rope, around 10 quick-draws
