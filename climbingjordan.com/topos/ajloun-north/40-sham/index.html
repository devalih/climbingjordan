Please keep this cliff an adventure-place with no bolts – use trad. gear or climb toprope!

Orientation: south

Grades: easy - medium

Potential: very little more

Rock quality: exellent limestone

Protection: trad. so far

Climbing-meters: 67m

Gear: 30m rope, around 10 quick-draws, nuts + cams, slings

GPS: 775m   N32° 24.690´  E35° 44.983´
